About the App

Youna is a simple, privacy-first period tracking app. It helps you log your cycle, track symptoms like mood, pain, and energy levels, and see predictions for upcoming periods. All your data stays on your device — no accounts, no cloud, no tracking.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a period?

Tap on a date in the calendar view to mark it as a period day. You can log the start of your period and the app will help you track the duration.

How do I track symptoms?

Select a day on the calendar and use the symptom panel to record your mood, pain level, and energy level for that day.

How are predictions calculated?

The app uses your logged period history to calculate your average cycle length, then predicts when your next period is likely to start. The more cycles you log, the more accurate predictions become.

Is my data backed up?

Your data is stored locally on your device. It is included in your standard iPhone backup (iCloud or computer backup) along with the rest of your app data. We do not have a separate cloud backup service.

How do I delete my data?

Delete the Youna app from your device. All data is permanently removed when the app is uninstalled.

Does the app work offline?

Yes — the app works entirely offline. It never connects to the internet.

What iOS version do I need?

Youna requires iOS 17.0 or later.
